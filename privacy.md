# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 26, 2026

## What We Collect
- Discord message IDs and channel IDs for quote functionality
- Discord server IDs and role IDs for configuration purposes

## What We Don't Collect
- Personal information
- Passwords or credentials
- Message content beyond what is explicitly saved via bot commands

## How Data Is Used
Collected data is used solely to operate bot features. No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

## Data Removal
To remove your server's data, kick the bot from your server. To request manual data removal, contact the bot owner.
